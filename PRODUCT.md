# PRODUCT.md

## Brand: Zaprill
Zaprill is a career intelligence platform designed for technical professionals. It provides real-time market data, resume analysis (ATS detection), and skill gap mapping to empower users in their career growth and salary negotiations.

## Users
- **Primary**: Software Engineers, Technical Leads, and Data Scientists in the Indian market.
- **Pain Points**: Lack of transparency in salary bands, silent rejections from ATS systems, and uncertainty about which skills truly move the needle for their next role.

## Tone & Voice
- **Professional**: Authoritative data sources.
- **Empowering**: Giving users the "data employers use".
- **Modern**: Sleek, tech-forward, high-signal, low-noise.
- **Direct**: No fluff. "Simple to understand" is a core value.

## Register: brand
The design should prioritize high-fidelity marketing impact. It needs to "wow" at first glance while maintaining the clarity of a utility tool.

## Anti-references
- Stock photos of smiling people in offices.
- Generic "Startup Blue" (#0070f3) as the only primary color.
- Flat, uninspiring card grids.
- Overly wordy marketing speak.
